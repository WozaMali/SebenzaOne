Frequently Asked Questions

Simply download our mobile app, create an account, and start collecting recyclable materials. Scan items to track your earnings and redeem rewards.

We accept PET bottles, aluminum cans, paper, cardboard, and various plastic materials. Check our app for the complete list of accepted items.

When you donate PET bottles to the Green Scholar program, the proceeds go directly to funding educational materials, scholarships, and school programs in Soweto.
